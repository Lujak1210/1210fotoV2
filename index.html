The Bug Gallery. Here is my second gallery, a collection of some of my favorite bug pictures. It was very tough to choose which ones would make the cut for this album. During the lockdown I had to get a little creative, the best part about having a yard is.......BUGS!. Green ones, Red ones, Crawly ones and Jumpy ones, wildlife in all sorts of form and color! All you have to do is slow down and
take a closer look.
